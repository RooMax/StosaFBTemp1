About Us

At the heart of our association lies a rich tapestry of shared experiences, deep-rooted friendships, and a commitment to upholding the values instilled in us during our time at St. Thomas' College. Founded with the purpose of fostering a strong bond among former scouts from the college, our association transcends geographical boundaries to connect St. Thomas' alumni across the globe.
